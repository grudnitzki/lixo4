This story is an X- Rated take-off an old hit T.V. series about three roomates...Sissy, Janet, and Jack. Their cozy sexual threesome becomes endangered by eviction when they fall behind in the rent, so they must hustle the only thing they know how to do for money. What follows is a comical account of their sexual endeavors. Along the way, Jack and Janet meet a rich, kindly socialite and a teenage hustler, while Sissy finds an eccentric playboy who think's he's Tarzan (John Holmes) and lives out his fantasy in the trees with her. After a day filled with hot erotic escapades, these three rommates find enough money to pay the rent, and more than enough new sensual tricks to keep teir threesome hot for a long time!
